Amazing DIY hacks

Outlining

At first draw an outline of your desired eyeliner style keeping your eyes OPEN.then slowly fill in the gaps and you're done. A perfect eyeliner will make your day more beautiful.

Sello Tape

Wanna have a perfect winged eyeliner? Yes,doing perfect winged eyeliner is not a tough jod if you have sellotapes. Just place the tape according to your desired anlge. Draw and fill in.

Bobby Pin

A bobby pin can really help you to a perfect winged eyeliner. Just colour the sharp edge of a bobby pin with your favourite eyeliner. Use the coloured bobbpy pin edge as an eyeliner wing stamp and you're good to go!

Spoon

Place a spoon at the corner of your eyes and draw the wing outline according your choice. Lastly fill the outlines and your perfectly perfect winged eyeliner is ready.
