About Us

"A thing of beauty is joy forever."

Beauty

    What is beauty? How do we define something as beautiful? These questions have been approached in so many ways throughout the ages and still there is no surefire way to describe beauty. Different people perceive beauty differently. For me, beauty is happiness . . . and confidence.

    Goals

    Beautification (or, generally termed as make-up) is important to me, because it not only makes me physically more attractive and appealing, but also help me showcase my inner-artist. It comforts me in a way that nothing else can. I know that many other people feel the same. Maybe some of them are stereotyped, some of them are confused — none of which is okay. So, to help these people in their odyssey of beauty, I have started this chase — the chase of my long-cherished dreams.
