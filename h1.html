Sello tape technique

Step 1

You need a tape to draw your winged eyeliner. Basically, first cut a small strip of tape and place it on your palm to reduce its stickiness.Then, place it on the corner of your eyes, as per your choice. Make sure it forms a proper angle with your eyebrow.

Step 2

Now, just draw a normal winged eyeliner and extend it towards the tape; you can even go over your tape. But be careful about the areas not having tape. Draw smooth lines where you've not place the tape.
