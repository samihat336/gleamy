Spoon trick

Step 1

Use the handle of the spoon to draw the angled line and the curve of the spoon to create the shape. Then fill it in to finish the flick.

Step 2

You can use spoon as a sheild while applying mascara. This spoon technique will keep your undereye area safe from your large mascara wand.

Step 3

That's how you create a winged eyeliner makeup with a help of a bobby pin. I liked how this trick works, the finished look is neat and pretty. If you messed up the process you can always clean the lines with a help of concealer.
