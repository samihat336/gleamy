Reuse your leftovers

Step 1

Old leftover lipstick tubes with some left in them(I am here using 2 Avon lipstick from simply pretty range) can be reused using this hack. Firstly,sanitize your cosmetic box and your spoon with the rubbing alcohol for hygiene purpose.

Step 2

Now you need to take your lipstick, scoop up the leftovers and Put it on the sanitized spoon. After that, lit the candle up and hold the spoon upon the candle. The lipstick will melt slowly within 30 to 40 seconds.

Step 3

Pour the liquid lipstick into your container. It will settle down within 15sec. Before using keep it into the refrigerator for 10 mins for better result. And yeah, your lip palette is ready.
