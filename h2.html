Outlining Eyeliner

Step 1

The very first step is to prep your eyelids. Put little amount of concealer on your eyelids. Blend it perfectly with a buffing brush or a beauty sponge. I'd recommend you to use your fingers, it works perfectly on eyelids. Set the concealer with loose powder or your favourite pressed powder.

Step 2

To have your winged eyeliner perfectly at first draw outlines. Pen eyeliner will work perfectly for this hack. Take a pen eyeliner and draw outlines of your desired eyeliner. Dotted outlines will help you to gat perfect shape and angle.

Step 3

Finally, join the dotted lines and also fill up the gaps. Complete the final step very carefully. If you're in a rust your eyeliner may get spoiled. Use dark pigmented eyeliner to fill the gaps to get more perfection. And you're good to go...
