DIY Face Mask

<

Step 1

Ingredients we need are:- 1 tablespoons of plain organic yogurt, 1 teaspoon of raw organic honey, 1 teaspoon of turmeric and 1 teaspoon of lemon juice-optional (great for lightening the skin and fighting oil). Mix the ingredients in a bowl, then slather it on your face with your fingers (or a brush) and let it sit for 15-20 minutes. Finally, rinse with lukewarm water and follow with your favorite moisturizer.

Step 2

Apply the mask in to your skin with a mask applicator so that the mask is appliend evenly. Be careful about your eyes. This mask may not cause inflamation but can create irritation in your eyes. Keep this mask until it fully dries.

Step 3

Rinse your face with lukewarm water and feel the difference. This mask gives your skin such a gorgeous glow, but go easy on the turmeric because it can stain your skin! The coconut oil gives your skin a hit of hydration while the honey and turmeric both possess anti-inflammatory and antibacterial properties that also help to smooth out your skin.
